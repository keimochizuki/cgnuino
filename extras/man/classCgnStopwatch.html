Detailed Description

In behavioral tasks, it is incredibly common for you to count elapsed time from a given origin. Being self-explanatory from its name, CgnStopwatch class works as a stopwatch for that faculty. After construction, call lap method to obtain the time length from the current origin, and then resetting the clock (i.e., set current time point as a new origin). Use get method to check the clock while maintaining the present origin.

Examples:
Lchika.ino, LchikaWave.ino, and SeqResp.ino.
